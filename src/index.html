Our Services

Services

Lizzards Preschool & Day care has been caring and educating children in the Lower Blue Mountains area since 1994. We provide a stimulating, safe and happy environment for children aged between 2 – 6 years. We are open from 7:00AM to 6:30PM Monday to Friday, 49 weeks per year.

Our Service is also fully accredited to provide you with both Child Care Benefit (CCB) and Childcare Cash Rebate (CCR) as a deduction in your fees.

Run by a former practicing midwife as well as a family health nurse, Mellissa Amirilayeghi has the knowledge and experience in ensuring your loved ones receive the best possible care and education during their stay at Lizzards.

As an added convenience, Lizzards also provides healthy, nutritious meals for all children. Meals are prepared and cooked on site daily. We cater for kids with special dietary and/or religious requirements.

Toddler Room

Educators in the Toddler room will plan, implement and evaluate activities for children’s learning based on the Early Years Learning Framework.

This framework conveys the highest expectations for all children’s learning from birth to five years through the following five learning outcomes:

  • Outcome 1: Children have a strong sense of identity.
  • Outcome 2: Children are connected with and contribute to their world.
  • Outcome 3: Children have a strong sense of wellbeing.
  • Outcome 4: Children are confident and involved learners.
  • Outcome 5: Children are effective communicators.

Preparing for School (pre school Program)

We understand that the transition to school is often a daunting prospect.

Will my child be ready, will they know enough, will they cope without me, what exactly do they need to know before they go?.

With this in mind, we approached our local schools to see what they felt children needed to know in order to ease the transition to formal education. Furthermore, we have a dedicated Early Learning educator who teaches our Preschool Program, based on the early Learning Framework curriculum. This ensures your child will commence school with a good understanding of what is academically expected.

Waitlist & Enrolement

Thank you for looking to book your child into Lizzards Preschool & Long Daycare! At Lizzards, we manage our waitlist enquiries online through the My Family Lounge (MFL) service. It takes next to no time to register. Once registered, it allows you to give us some basic information and place your child in our waitlist.

If you have requested a permanent booking, our centre processes these enquiries usually within 48 hours.

It's easy to Enrol in our service, simply follow these 3 steps:

Step 1

For NEW families to My Family Lounge (MFL), click on the REGISTER button. You will then be taken through a registration process to create your My Family Lounge (MFL) account.

Step 2

Log into your My Family Lounge (MFL) account and tell us about you, your child and what bookings you are after so we can help you out.

Step 3

If you have requested permanent bookings, we will come back to you within 48hours and advise if we have a spot available for your child. If we have a spot, we will email you an offer and ask you to accept the offer and complete and submit the Enrolement form in the MFL portal.

As a bonus, once your enrolement is confirmed at our centre, you can also request ‘Casual booking days’ for your child, all via the MyFamily portal or App! You simply check our availability via the app and submit and confirm your booking – Gone are the days of calling the centre in the morning and hoping for a position to be available for your child!

Fun and educational activities are organised by our educators every day. This included fun time outdoors, where children play and explore within our grounds, under the supervision and guidance of our educators.

Our indoor activities range from arts, science, reading and basic arithmetic. All areas of development, (fine motor, gross motor, social, emotional, language and cognitive) are incorporated throughout the day within a range of activities set out within leaning centres both indoors and outdoors. Here children are able to make choices in their play and choose from a variety of materials and mediums.

About us

Lizzards Preschool & Long Day care is a family owned and operated business, aiming to provide the highest standards of care and early childhood development for children from two years to six years of age. We also aim to provide an environment and conditions where staff can flourish in their role as educators of young children.

Our centre is located within pristine bushland setting in Sydney’s Lower Blue Mountains area of Hawkesbury Heights. Our outdoor playground is filled with beautifully maintained Australian native plants and trees, allowing the children to play, explore and learn. We also have many native birds visiting us every day! Children are able to observe and enjoy these beautiful animals in their natural habitat, teaching them about the importance of conservation.
